Bike Path to FZJ

Here’s a nice shot of the bike path I used to take day to and from Forschungszentrum Jülich. Riding along this safe and beautiful path was a great start and end to each workday! I really loved bike-riding in Germany. The infrastructure to do so was spectacular.

Especially during my last few weeks in Jülich, I often sat at the bench hidden behind that middle tree to take in and appreciate my limited time left in Germany and to reflect on my summer. It was a nice routine after work.
